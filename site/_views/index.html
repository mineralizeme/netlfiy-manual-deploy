It's All About The Ocean.

The research done in 1938 by Dr. Maynard Murray was a game-changer.

read more

Farming practices do not include enough minerals in the soil to grow healthy plants. Common fertilizers like NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) do not have all of the trace minerals necessary for strong immune systems to help fight disease.

Re-Mineralizing the topsoil to include all of the minerals necessary to grow health plants is our mission. When all trace minerals are bio-available from nature, plants are disease-resistant and the need for pesticide and insecticide disappears.
‚Äç
Luckily for us, all of the earth's minerals end up in one place: the ocean. We can use the regenerative power of the sea to re-mineralize farming soil by collecting dehydrated sea salt from beds located in nutrient-dense areas around the world.

To be clear: this is not watering your plants with ocean water. However, naturally occurring sea salt in its purest form contains minerals in their ionic form, making it more absorbable for plants. Ocean water mineral fertilizer is meant to be diluted and applied at specified concentrations and dosages.
